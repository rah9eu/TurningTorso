------------------------------Overview of what each folder contains------------------------------
Animations:  	    Contains the two animations I created, as well as the GrassHopper scripts 
		    used to create them. Both animations are 960x540p.
Final Model: 	    Contains the final daytime and nighttime models of the Turning Torso
Model Construction: Contains a model from before my GrassHopper scripts were ran, as well as 
		    the result after they ran. Both GrassHopper scripts are also included in this
		    folder.
Still Renders:	    Contains seven 1920x1080p renderings of my final model
